FOREVA PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy is effective 15 Oct, 2018 and governs your use of the software application FOREVA (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by CROWD COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY PVT LTD. The Application is a hub for User Generated Content around user Interests and Preferences. On basis of explicitly provided and implicitly inferred interests of each user, this application searches the Internet for relevant User Generated Content in formats including, but not limited to, posts, comments, photos, videos, audio files, ratings, reviews, discussion threads, and blogs, presents the same in different insightful ways to allow meaningful consumption and browsing of such content, and facilitates conversations around each such topic amongst its user community who may all be interested in the same topics.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

Registration with us is via external social networking platforms, and is mandatory in order to be able to use the basic features of the Application. The Application seeks and obtains information about you when you download and register the Application. Please see the section below to see what information we automatically collect and how, during registration.

After you register with us and use the Application, you generally provide (a) transaction and activity related information, such as when you make purchases, respond to any offers, click through to external links, like, upvote or downvote content in the Application, or download or use applications from or via us; (b) metadata such as time, date and location of actions for any action you take using our Application, such as clicking a picture or posting a video; (c) information you provide us when you contact us for help, and; (d) information you enter into our system when using the Application, including, but not limited to interests, contact information, demographic information, posts, comments, ratings, shared attachments or links and messages.

We may use the information you provided us to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information

At the time of Registration, the Application collects information from and/or for social networking platforms (e.g., contact information, friends’ lists, login information, photos or check-ins). We always ensure that your express prior consent is obtained, when we do this. WE WILL NEVER POST TO ANY SOCIAL NETWORKING PLATFORM ANYTHING ON YOUR BEHALF, UNLESS YOU EXPRESSLY CONSENT AND GIVE US PERMISSION TO DO SO.

Upon registration, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

When you visit the mobile application, we may use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine your current location in order to determine the city and locality you are located within, serve you location relevant information, display presence of nearby communities and location maps with relevant advertisements. If you do not want us to use your location for the purposes set forth above, you should turn off the location services for the mobile application located in your account settings or in your mobile phone settings and/or within the mobile application.
